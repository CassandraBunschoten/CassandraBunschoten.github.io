Project

Master Thesis
From September 2020 till June 2021 I will be working on my thesis titled 'Using spatio-temporal CAR priors to increase sensitivity in EEG analyses'. My thesis focusses on the multiple testing problem in EEG research, and tries to solve this by utilising a Bayesian framework. I will be combining methods from epidemiological disease mapping with EEG. The goal is to take the first steps in improving EEG analyses.
